How does always allowing public members of organization work?

If If a GitHub organization is included in the list to be always allowed, public members of this organization will not be required to sign a CLA. We retrieve the list of public members of an organization to check this.

Why always allow an organization?

We see at least two use cases you may want to do so:
  • You want your own organization members to not have to sign a CLA.
  • You have a CLA in place with another organization and allowing their organization ensures that the individuals don't have to sign a CLA.
